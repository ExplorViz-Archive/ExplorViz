Welcome to the ExplorViz tutorial.<br>
You will be introduced to the main features of ExplorViz, 
so that you can complete the experiment without problems.<br>
Use the mouse wheel or +/- on your keyboard to zoom in and out. To move the landscape, you can either drag it with the left mouse button or use WASD or the arrow keys.<br>
<br>
To make a software landscape more clearly arranged, you can minimize systems 
to only show their name but not their applications. 
This is indicated with the - in the top right corner.<br>
<br>
To complete the first tutorial step, minimize the OCD Editor by double clicking it.
