The software landscape consists of several <b>systems</b> (grey), and the <b>communication</b> between them (orange lines). Thicker lines mean more communication.<br>
<br>
To get a better overview over a landscape, it can be helpful to <b>minimize</b> the systems, so they take up less space. The ability to do so is indicated by the - in the top right corner.<br>
<br>
<b>To complete the first tutorial step, minimize the OCD Editor by double clicking it.</b>
