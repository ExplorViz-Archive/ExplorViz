The systems are made up of <span style="color:#00c444; font-weight:bold">nodes</span>. For example, you can see node <b>10.0.0.3</b> and <b>10.0.1.1</b>.<br> 
<br>
In addition, nodes can be part of a <span style="color:#15992a; font-weight:bold">node group</span>. Node groups consist of duplicated nodes and applications.
