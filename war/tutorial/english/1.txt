This is the application visualization of a mockup of Neo4j. <br>
Here you can see the internals of the application.
<br>
<br>
Packages are colored <span style="color:#00bf42; font-weight:bold">green</span>.<br>
<br>
The blocks represent <b>closed packages</b>, e.g., the package <b>lifecycle</b>.<br>
The platforms represent <b>opened packages</b>, which show their contained subpackages and classes (if existing), e.g., the package <b>graphdb</b>. <br>
