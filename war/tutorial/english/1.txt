This is the application visualization of a mockup of Neo4j. <br>
Here you can see the internals of the application.
<br>
<br>
The <span style="color:#159d2b; font-weight:bold">dark green</span> blocks represent <b>closed packages</b>.<br>
The <span style="color:#00bf42; font-weight:bold">light green</span> platforms represent <b>opened packages</b>, which show their contained subpackages and classes (if existing). <br>
