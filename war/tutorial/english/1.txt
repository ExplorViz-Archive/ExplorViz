The software landscape consists of several <span style="color:#c7c7c7; font-weight:bold">systems</span>.<br>
<br>
To get a better overview over a landscape, it can be helpful to <b>minimize</b> the systems, so they take up less space. The ability to do so is indicated by the - in the top right corner.<br>
<br>
<b>To complete the first tutorial step, minimize the OCD Editor by double clicking its gray frame.</b>
