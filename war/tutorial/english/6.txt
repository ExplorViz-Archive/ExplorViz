Many communications can make it difficult to get an overview over those communications, that currently are of interest for you. To avoid this, you can <b>left click</b> on a class once. All communication lines that don't belong to it will then turn transparent. <br>
To find out what happens when a software is started, you can identify the main class and take a look at the communications belonging to it.
