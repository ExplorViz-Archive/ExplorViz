This is the application visualization. <br>
<br>
Here you can see the internal communication of an application. The <span style="color:#00c444; font-weight:bold">green</span> blocks represent <b>packages</b>, <span style="color:#6848b4; font-weight:bold">purple</span> blocks represent <b>classes</b>.<br>
<br>
The <span style="color:#ff981d; font-weight:bold">orange</span> lines represent the communication between the classes and packages. The thicker the line, the more communication happens.<br>
