This is the application visualisation. <br>
Here you can see the internal communication of an application. The light green blocks represent <b>components</b>, purple ones represent <b>classes</b>.<br>
The orange lines represent the communication between the classes and components. The thicker the lines, the more communication happes.<br>
<br>
Communication with other applications is shown as incoming and outgoing communication that ends in black figures to represent the other application.
