To quickly gain basic information about classes, packages, or communications, you can <b>hover</b> with the mouse over them.<br>
<span style="color:#00c444; font-weight:bold">Packages</span> give information about the amount of contained classes and packages.
The <span style="color:#6848b4; font-weight:bold">class</span> information tells you the amount of active instances and called methods.<br>
<br>
<b>To continue, hover the cursor over the main package.</b>
