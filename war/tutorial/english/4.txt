Hovering over a class, package or communication will open a popover giving additional information to quickly get a better understand of the program.<br>
The class information tells you the amount of active instances and called methods. <br>
Components give information about the amount of classes and packages they consist of.<br>
Hovering over communications shows the names of the caller and callee and lists all method calls.<br>
