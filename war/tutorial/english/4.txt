Hovering over a class, package or communication will open a popover giving additional information to quickly get a better understand of the program.<br>
The <span style="color:#6848b4; font-weight:bold">class</span> information tells you the amount of active instances and called methods. <br>
<span style="color:#00c444; font-weight:bold">Packages</span> give information about the amount of classes and packages they consist of.<br>
